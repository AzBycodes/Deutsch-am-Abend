About Us

Empowering language learners since 2015

Our Mission

At Deutsch am Abend, we believe that learning a language is more than memorizing grammar rules and vocabulary—it's about opening doors to new cultures, opportunities, and perspectives. Since 2015, we've been dedicated to providing high-quality German language education that empowers our students to achieve their personal and professional goals.

Our comprehensive curriculum follows the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR), ensuring that your qualifications are recognized internationally. Whether you're learning German for work, study, travel, or personal enrichment, we provide the structure, support, and expertise you need to succeed.

Our Approach

We combine proven pedagogical methods with modern teaching techniques to create an engaging and effective learning environment. Our courses emphasize:

  • Communicative Competence: We prioritize practical language skills that you can use in real-world situations
  • Cultural Context: Understanding German culture enriches your language learning experience
  • Interactive Learning: Small class sizes ensure personalized attention and active participation
  • Structured Progress: Clear learning objectives and regular assessments track your advancement
  • Exam Preparation: Specialized courses to help you succeed in official certification exams

Why Choose Us

Our experienced instructors are near-native German speakers with professional teaching qualifications. They bring passion, expertise, and cultural insights to every lesson, creating a supportive atmosphere where students feel confident to practice and make mistakes—an essential part of language learning.

We maintain small class sizes (8-15 students for regular courses, maximum 10 for exam preparation) to ensure that each student receives individual attention and has ample opportunity to participate actively in class.

Our track record speaks for itself: over 99% of our exam preparation students pass their certification exams on their first attempt, and our alumni have gone on to study at German universities, work for German companies, and integrate successfully into German-speaking communities.
